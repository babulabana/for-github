BCA

Bachelor of Computer Applications

Computers were invented long back in 1940’s, but in today’s techno-savvy era, individuals use it in each and every aspect of personal or professional world. As a result, there is a Spectacular demand of IT professionals to satisfy various IT requirements which provide not only lucrative package but secure professional growth.

BCA also known as Bachelors in Computer Application is a 3 years undergraduate degree course (6 semesters) which is considered as foundation of entering into IT industry in companies such as TCS (Tata Consultancy Service), Infosys, Amazon, Wipro, Capgemini. Apart from that student can get job in IT Department of Government offices. This foundation course itself ensured graduates to secure better job with decent package at initial stage of their career. It is so flexible that it allows HSC (12th grade) students from various streams like Science, Commerce or Art (For ART’s there should be a pre-university education completed in order to pursue BCA) to opt for this course.
